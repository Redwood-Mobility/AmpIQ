Process

From first call to a live charging program.

You do not need to become an EV charging expert to make a good decision. The process is designed to keep the complexity with us.

Step 01

Discovery call

We align on the property, goals, usage expectations, budget, and timing, then determine the right next level of diligence.

Step 02

Site assessment

Electrical capacity, parking flow, utility interconnection, and permitting realities get mapped before design work begins.

Step 03

System design

We define charger quantity, placement, hardware, software, and phased rollout strategy based on the actual operating model.

Step 04

Incentive and rebate capture

Applicable programs are identified early so they can shape decisions rather than arrive as an afterthought.

Step 05

Procurement and installation

We coordinate hardware sourcing, installer relationships, commissioning, and the sequence required to launch with fewer surprises.

Step 06

Handoff or ongoing management

Take ownership of a turnkey system or keep AmpIQ involved for monitoring, maintenance coordination, and operational optimization.
