What We Do

End-to-end EV charging consulting

From initial site assessment to ongoing network management, AmpIQ orchestrates every phase of your charging infrastructure project.

Site Assessment

We evaluate your property's electrical capacity, parking layout, utility requirements, and permitting landscape to build a realistic project plan.

System Design

Hardware-agnostic recommendations tailored to your property. We spec the right mix of L2 and L3 chargers, software platform, and phasing strategy.

Incentive Capture

We identify and pursue every applicable federal, state, utility, and local incentive to minimize your out-of-pocket investment.

Procurement and Installation

Ongoing Management

Network monitoring, maintenance coordination, revenue optimization, and reporting. We keep your chargers running and generating revenue.

Hardware Agnostic
